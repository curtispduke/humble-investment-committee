# Humble Round Table

> Here's something worth understanding today.

The world doesn't need more opinions.

It needs better conversations.

Humble Round Table is a community built around evidence, reasoning, curiosity, and respectful disagreement.

You don't need credentials.
You don't need to be an expert.
You don't need to pretend you already know.

Bring one good question.

We'll learn together.

One question at a time.

---

## Why we exist

We believe curiosity should never be intimidating.

Everyone deserves permission to ask questions.

Permission to not know.

Permission to change their mind.

Permission to challenge experts.

Permission to think.

Our goal isn't to tell people what to think.

Our goal is to help people think more clearly by making evidence, reasoning, and respectful disagreement accessible to everyone.

---

## Principles

- Seek truth, not confirmation.
- Understanding before certainty.
- Primary sources over headlines.
- Strong opinions, loosely held.
- Every assumption can be challenged.
- Every decision should be documented.
- Learn together.
- One question at a time.

---

## The Round Table

Every discussion begins with an empty chair.

You decide who sits at the table.

Bring an investor.

Bring a scientist.

Bring a teacher.

Bring someone younger than you.

Bring someone older than you.

Bring someone who disagrees with you.

No one stays forever.

People join the conversation when their perspective is useful, then make room for someone else.

The goal isn't consensus.

The goal is understanding.
